Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Bucket Budget values your privacy. We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how the app works.

1. Data Collection

Bucket Budget does not collect or store any personal data on external servers. All budget data remains securely on your device.

2. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to third parties. Your financial information stays private.

3. Local Storage

Your inputs (categories, transactions, totals) are stored only locally on your iPhone. If you delete the app, the data is removed as well.

4. Analytics

Bucket Budget does not use tracking tools or advertising frameworks.

5. Contact

If you have privacy-related questions, please contact us at support@bucketbudget.app.
